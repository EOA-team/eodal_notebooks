Times in UTC:            0040 UTC = 02:40 Summer time
                                  = 01:40 Winter time

Observation interval for hourly values, unless otherwise indicated in the parameter descrip-tion: HH  = (HH-1):01 - HH:00
Example: 13 = observation period 12:01 to 13:00 

Missing values are marked with '-'
